About SPEAK

(Speaking Promotes Education And Knowledge)

SPEAK provides multiple free fun activities, ideas and information to support and nurture oral language development in children 0-6. A strong foundation in oral language (the ability to effectively communicate through speaking and listening) is essential for successful learning and its influence lasts a lifetime.

SPEAK is for everyone who wants the best start in life for their children. Access the age buttons on the home screen to begin your adventure.

*Children grow and develop at different rates. The information in this app is offered as a guide only. If you have any concerns discuss them with your doctor or healthcare nurse.
